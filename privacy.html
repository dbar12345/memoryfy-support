Privacy Policy

MemoryFy — AI Memory Aid & Caregiver Hub

Effective Date: February 1, 2025

Last Updated: January 31, 2025

MemoryFy Inc ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MemoryFy mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

By using MemoryFy, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Elder users: Anonymous sign-in (no personal credentials required). Optionally, name and profile photo.
  • Caregiver users: Email address, name, phone number (optional), and profile photo (optional).
  • Social sign-in: If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your name and email from those services.

Health Data

With your explicit permission, MemoryFy accesses Apple HealthKit data including:

  • Step count, walking distance, and active calories
  • Heart rate and resting heart rate
  • Blood oxygen saturation
  • Sleep duration and quality
Health data is only collected when you enable health tracking in Settings. You control which specific metrics are shared with caregivers.

Location Data

With your permission, we collect location data to:

  • Attach GPS coordinates to memories in your Memory Vault
  • Share your current location with authorized caregivers for safety
  • Detect whether you are at your home location
Location sharing is opt-in. You control the update frequency (real-time, hourly, or on-demand) and can disable it at any time.

Photos and Voice Notes

  • Photos: Captured via camera or selected from your photo library when creating memories, setting a profile picture, or verifying medication completion.
  • Voice notes: Audio recordings you create for memories. Voice data for commands is processed on-device and is not stored permanently.

Usage and Activity Data

  • Reminder creation, completion, and missed events
  • Memory access patterns
  • Brain game scores and completion data
  • Voice command usage (processed on-device)

This data is used for smart suggestions and pattern recognition to improve your experience. Activity data older than 90 days is automatically deleted.

2. How We Use Your Information

Data Purpose
Account info Create and manage your account, enable caregiver connections
Health metrics Display health dashboard, share with authorized caregivers
Location Memory tagging, caregiver safety monitoring
Photos & voice notes Memory vault storage and retrieval
Activity patterns Smart suggestions, optimal reminder timing, caregiver alerts
Brain game data Track cognitive exercise progress, adjust difficulty
We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

With Caregivers

If you are an elder user, your authorized caregivers may access:

  • Reminder status and activity summaries
  • Health metrics (if you enable health sharing)
  • Location data (if you enable location sharing)
  • Memory vault items (based on permissions you set)

You control caregiver permissions through granular settings. You can revoke caregiver access at any time.

With Service Providers

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database (Firestore), file storage, and push notifications. Data is stored on Google Cloud servers.
  • Apple HealthKit: Health data is read locally on your device. We do not send raw HealthKit data to any external server beyond Firebase for caregiver sharing.
  • Google Sign-In / Facebook Login: Used only for authentication. We receive your name and email to create your account.

With Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Firebase Analytics is disabled in our app.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely using Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Photos and voice notes are stored in Firebase Storage with user-specific access controls.
  • Voice command processing (speech recognition) is performed on-device using Apple Speech framework and on-device AI models. Voice command audio is not stored or transmitted.
  • Caregiver access is enforced through Firebase security rules tied to established care relationships.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Activity logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days.
  • Memories, reminders, and photos: Retained until you delete them or close your account.
  • Health and location data: Overwritten with each update; historical snapshots are not retained.

6. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Health sharing: Enable or disable per metric in Settings.
  • Location sharing: Control frequency or disable entirely.
  • Caregiver permissions: Grant or revoke specific permissions for each caregiver.
  • Delete memories and reminders: Remove any stored data at any time.
  • Delete account: Contact us to request full account deletion, including all associated data.
  • Device permissions: Revoke camera, microphone, location, or HealthKit access through iOS Settings at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

MemoryFy is designed for elderly users and their adult caregivers. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Apple HealthKit Compliance

In compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:

  • Health data accessed through HealthKit is not used for advertising or marketing.
  • Health data is not sold to data brokers or third parties.
  • Health data is only shared with caregivers when the user has explicitly granted permission.
  • Health data is stored securely and transmitted using encryption.

9. Tracking and App Tracking Transparency

MemoryFy may request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission if you choose to sign in with Facebook. This is required by Facebook's SDK for authentication. You can deny this permission and still use Facebook login with limited features, or choose an alternative sign-in method.

We do not use tracking for advertising or analytics purposes. Firebase Analytics is disabled in our app.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
